# Commercial Use

Planner's PPT Hell is open source under AGPL-3.0. The community edition is designed to be useful, inspectable, and modifiable.

Commercial support and licensing are available separately for teams that need private deployment, closed-source integration, or customized PPT production workflows.

## Available Commercial Work

- private workflow deployment
- enterprise template adaptation
- proposal and pitch deck production pipelines
- custom validators and quality gates
- private review dashboards
- internal training for agent-assisted PPT workflows
- closed-source commercial licensing

## When To Contact

Contact the maintainer if you want to:

- integrate Planner's PPT Hell into a closed-source product;
- offer a hosted service based on a modified version;
- build a private company version with non-public templates or scripts;
- use the project name, author identity, or brand in commercial promotion;
- commission a custom PPT workflow for a team or client.
